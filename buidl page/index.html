About Us

The BUIDL Crew is a community of blockchain enthusiasts, developers, and builders. Our mission is to provide education, support, and resources to empower individuals and organizations in their blockchain journey. We worked with note-worthy Web3 players like Polkadot, Binance, NEAR, Polygon, the State Government of Telangana and more, to conduct 50+ innovation programmes to encourage developers to BUIDL. Our efforts helped over 60,000 builders with a collective US $10M through grants and prizes to support their innovation journeys
